    "## Wisdom on tuples and lists\n",
    "\n",
    "At face, tuples and lists are very similar, differing essentially only in mutability. The differences are actually more profound, as described in the [aforementioned blog post](http://www.asmeurer.com/blog/posts/tuples/). We will make extensive use of them in our programs.  \n",
    "\n",
    "\"When should I use a tuple and when should I use a list?\" you ask.  Here is my advice.\n",
    "\n",
    "<div style=\"color: dodgerblue; text-align: center; font-weight: bold;\">\n",
    "\n",
    "Always use tuples instead of lists unless you need mutability.\n",
    "    \n",
    "</div>\n",
    "\n",
    "This keeps you out of trouble. It is very easy to inadvertently change one list, and then another list (that is actually the same, but with a different variable name) gets mangled. However, after you have finalized your list, you should convert it to a tuple so it cannot get mangled. We'll come back to this later in the bootcamp.\n",
