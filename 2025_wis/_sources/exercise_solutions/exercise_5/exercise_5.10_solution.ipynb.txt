    "**d)** If $m$ is the mass of bacteria, which is proportional to the area in the images, then, for exponential growth,\n",
    "\n",
    "\\begin{align}\n",
    "m(t) = m_0\\,\\mathrm{e}^{rt},\n",
    "\\end{align}\n",
    "\n",
    "where $r$ is the growth rate. Taking the logarithm of both sides gives\n",
    "\n",
    "\\begin{align}\n",
    "\\ln m(t) = \\ln m_0 + rt.\n",
    "\\end{align}\n",
    "\n",
    "So, the slope of the line on a log-log scale is the growth rate.\n",
    "\n",
    "With this in mind, plot the bacterial area as a function of time with the area on a log scale. If the plot is linear, the bacteria are performing exponential growth."
   ]
